Project Overview
The project is based on the concept of digital literacy. Digital literacy is important for students in today’s technology-savvy world. As a student digital ambassador, the aim of this project is to learn and demonstrate the proper, safe, and efficient use of digital technology. The project has five tasks related to various aspects of digital literacy. The aspects of digital literacy covered in this project are awareness, professional online presence, coding and collaboration tools, communication etiquette, and cyber safety.

In this project, I have designed an infographic related to digital literacy awareness. I have also created my professional online presence on various platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. I have also learned about coding tools like Google Forms and other coding platforms. I have also learned to write professional emails. I have also learned the importance of proper usage of social media. In addition to this, I have also learned about cybercrime like UPI fraud. I have also created a case study related to it. In this project, I have learned various aspects of digital literacy.
