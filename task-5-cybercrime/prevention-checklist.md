# Prevention Checklist

1. General Safety Tips

* Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
* Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.
* Don’t click on suspicious links or unknown attachments.
* Keep your devices updated (OS, apps, antivirus) to avoid security vulnerabilities.
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking or logging into important accounts.
* Check app permissions before installing apps; avoid unnecessary access to contacts, camera, etc.

2. Financial / UPI Safety Tips

* Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.
* Double-check the receiver’s UPI ID or QR code before sending money.
* Do not approve “Collect Requests” unless you trust the sender and understand the purpose.
* Avoid downloading unknown apps or screen-sharing apps suggested by strangers claiming to be support agents.

2. Social Media & Personal Safety

* Set your social media profiles to private and review follower lists regularly.
* Avoid sharing live location, travel plans, or personal details publicly.
* Be cautious of fake job/internship offers asking for money or personal data.

2. Report Cyber Crimes

If you face fraud, harassment, or any cybercrime:

* Report online: National Cyber Crime Portal = https://cybercrime.gov.in
* Call helpline: 1930
