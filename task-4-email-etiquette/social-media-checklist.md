

Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

do's

Be polite and respectful in your comments and posts.
Check the facts before you share them (don't spread false information).
Keep your personal information safe.
Before you post, think about how it will affect you in the long run.
Use strong passwords and turn on two-factor authentication.

Don'ts
Do not bully or harass people online.
Don't share information that isn't true or that you haven't checked.
Don't share too much personal or private information.
Don't put up content that is rude or inappropriate.
Don't accept requests to be friends or follow people you don't know.
